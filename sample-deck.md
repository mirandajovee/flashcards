# Grounding & Dissociation

## Interventions for Dissociation
#### Look for the circles in the squares.
#### Press feet into the floor and name the contact.
#### Hold something cold and describe it out loud.

## Orienting to the Present
#### Name five things you can see, four you can hear, three you can touch.
#### Notice the temperature of the room on your skin.

## Window of Tolerance
#### Notice where you are: hyper-aroused, settled, or shut down.
#### Name it gently, without judgment.
#### Choose one small action to widen the window.
